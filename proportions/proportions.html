Fashionable Silhouettes

Almost any outfit that is fashionable right now (2024) will follow one of four sets of ratios.

Choosing a silhouette (set of ratios) is the first step in creating an outfit.

Many people do everything right: they get a collection of fashionable, amazing items all which may individually have fashionable proportions.

But they then fail to put them together in the ratios laid out by one of these 4 silhouettes.

Inversely, some people will have unfashionable, out-of-style items and put very little thought into their fashion choices.

But they will naturally put together outfits in accordance with these silhouettes and be fashionable effortlessly and inexplicably
